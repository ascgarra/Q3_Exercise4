Honkong

Hongkong is one of the Special Administrative Regions (SAR) in China. Located on the southern coast of China, Hong Kong occupies a strategic location in mainland China and other neighboring countries. As a result, Hong Kong has been the site of many battles and conflicts in its past. Today, Hong Kong is a prosperous and modern city with plenty of amenities and attractions to offer visitors.

Activities to do in HongKong: Visit Disneyland, Tian Tan Buddha, Ocean Park and many more!

Being in Hongkong was like living in a dream. Even though it was a short vacation, we had so much fun. Those days cleared my mind seeing one of my favorite cartoons in Disneyland, Ocean Park was also a memorable experience and Tian Tian Buddha was also fun but very tiring to climb the stairs. If you go to Hongkong you better wear comfy shoes and ready your feet, there's a lot of walking for sure.
